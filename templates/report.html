📌 RFM Methodology

Recency (R) — How recently a customer purchased. Lower days = higher score.
Frequency (F) — How many times they ordered. More orders = higher score.
Monetary (M) — Total spend. Higher value = higher score.

Each dimension is scored 1–4 and summed. Champions (10–12) are your most engaged, high-spending, recent buyers. Lost (3–4) customers need urgent win-back efforts.
